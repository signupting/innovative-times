Why Invest in the Enterprise Investment Scheme (EIS)?

The EIS is designed to help smaller, higher-risk companies raise finance by offering a range of tax reliefs to investors who purchase new shares in those companies.

30% Income Tax Relief

Claim up to £300,000 income tax relief per year on investments up to £1 million.

Tax-Free Growth

No Capital Gains Tax on profits from the sale of shares after they have been held for three years.

Inheritance Tax Relief

After two years, EIS shares are typically exempt from Inheritance Tax.

Loss Protection

Offset losses against your income tax bill if the company you invest in fails.
