Investment Disclaimer

Last updated: May 2025

Important Notice: The content on this website is for informational purposes only and does not constitute financial, investment, or tax advice. Investing in EIS and SEIS schemes carries significant risks, including the potential loss of your investment.

Risk Warning

Before making any investment decisions, please consider the following risks:

  • The value of investments can go down as well as up
  • Past performance is not a reliable indicator of future results
  • Early-stage and small company investments are particularly high risk
  • EIS and SEIS investments are illiquid and difficult to sell
  • Tax treatment depends on individual circumstances and may change in the future

Not Financial Advice

The Innovative Times does not provide personal investment advice. We strongly recommend that you:

  • Seek professional financial advice before making any investment decisions
  • Only invest what you can afford to lose
  • Carefully read all offering documents before investing
  • Consider your investment objectives and risk tolerance

Tax Relief

While EIS and SEIS investments offer potential tax benefits, eligibility for tax relief depends on:

  • Individual circumstances
  • Company maintaining its qualifying status
  • Holding the investment for the required period
  • HMRC rules and regulations
