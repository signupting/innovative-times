Regulatory Information

Last updated: May 2025

Important Notice: The Innovative Times is not authorized or regulated by the Financial Conduct Authority (FCA). We do not provide investment advice or arrange investments.

Our Role

The Innovative Times operates as an independent information provider:

  • We provide educational content about EIS and SEIS investments
  • We do not arrange investments or provide investment advice
  • We are not a financial advisor or investment manager
  • We do not handle client money or assets

EIS and SEIS Regulations

EIS and SEIS schemes are regulated by:

  • HM Revenue & Customs (HMRC)
  • The Financial Conduct Authority (FCA)

Companies seeking EIS/SEIS investment must meet strict eligibility criteria set by HMRC.

Investor Protection

As we are not regulated by the FCA:

  • You will not have access to the Financial Ombudsman Service
  • You will not be protected by the Financial Services Compensation Scheme (FSCS)
  • You should seek regulated financial advice before making investment decisions

Financial Promotions

Our content:

  • Is for information and educational purposes only
  • Does not constitute a financial promotion
  • Is not intended to influence investment decisions
  • Should not be relied upon as investment advice

Complaints Procedure

While we are not regulated, we take complaints seriously:

  • All complaints will be investigated thoroughly
  • We aim to respond within 14 working days
  • Complaints can be submitted via email
